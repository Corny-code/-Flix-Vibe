"Despicable Me-4"

Synopsis:

Gru, Lucy, Margo, Edith, and Agnes welcome a new member to the family, Gru Jr., who is intent on tormenting his dad. Gru faces a new nemesis in Maxime Le Mal and his girlfriend Valentina, and the family is forced to go on the run.
The movie begins with Gru attending a reunion at his old school, Lychee Pas Bon. Whilst there, he reconciles with his old foe Maxime Le Mal, who had his act stolen by Gru as a child and has plotted revenge ever since. Maxime says that he has used cockroach parts to make him more powerful, in a bid to conquer the entire world, but Gru along with the rest of the AVL, arrests him. Unfortunately, with the help of his girlfriend Valentina, Maxime escapes from prison and shows his intention of turning the human race into human-cockroach hybrids. Maxime plans to try it out on Grus biological son, Gru Jr.
